Who we are

MoodShift: CBT AI Coach ("MoodShift", "we", "our") is an iOS mental wellness application. MoodShift uses Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques delivered through an AI-powered coaching interface — available as text sessions and Voice AI sessions.

MoodShift is not a medical device and does not provide clinical therapy, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a self-help wellness tool designed to support emotional reflection and cognitive reframing.

What we collect

Data stored locally on your device

✓ Stays on your iPhone — never uploaded
  • CBT session conversations and messages
  • Mood scores (1–10) from completed sessions
  • AI memory summaries — only if you enable "AI Coach Personalization"
  • Session history, type (text or Voice AI), and completion status
  • App preferences (language, theme, disclaimer acceptance)

Data processed by AI (not stored)

Anthropic Claude API

Your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate CBT coaching responses. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored or used for AI training under our API agreement. Sessions pass through our Cloudflare Workers proxy which applies rate limiting only — no content logging.

Voice AI sessions (Voice AI plan only)

Speech Recognition & Synthesis — fully on-device

Voice sessions use Apple's on-device Speech Recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer). Your voice is processed locally — audio is not sent to external servers. Transcribed text is sent to the Claude API. AI responses are spoken using Apple's AVSpeechSynthesizer, also processed entirely on-device. No audio recordings are stored.

iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in History, your session data is stored in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. Apple's privacy policy governs this storage. You can disable sync and delete all iCloud data at any time from within the app.

Purchase information

Premium and Voice AI subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. We receive only subscription status confirmation via StoreKit 2.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage tracking
  • Audio recordings from Voice AI sessions

How we use your data

  • To provide AI CBT coaching responses during text and voice sessions
  • To display your session history and mood trends within the app
  • To personalize future sessions using "AI Coach Personalization" (only when enabled by you)
  • To enforce session limits for Free plan users (3 sessions/day)
  • To verify subscription status for Premium and Voice AI features

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app's features.

Your rights & controls

  • Delete sessions: Swipe left on any session in History to delete permanently
  • Disable AI memory: Uncheck "AI Coach Personalization" before starting a session
  • Disable iCloud sync: Tap the iCloud icon in History — disabling deletes all cloud copies
  • Delete all data: Delete the app to remove all local data permanently
  • Manage subscription: Cancel anytime via App Store → Account → Subscriptions

As MoodShift requires no account, there is no profile to delete. All your data exists only on your device and optionally in your own iCloud account.

Mental health & sensitive data

Our commitment

CBT sessions may contain deeply personal thoughts and feelings. This is exactly why we built MoodShift with a local-first architecture — your most sensitive data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose iCloud sync.

MoodShift is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health crisis line in your country.

Children's privacy

MoodShift is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used MoodShift, please contact us.

GDPR & international users

  • Legal basis for AI processing: Contractual necessity (to deliver the coaching service)
  • No cross-border transfers of personal data directly by us
  • Right to erasure: delete the app and all local data is removed immediately
  • No systematic personal data processing requiring a DPO

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in features or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notice. The "Effective" date at the top indicates the last update.
