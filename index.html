Who we are

MoodShift: CBT AI Coach ("MoodShift", "we", "our") is an iOS mental wellness application. MoodShift uses Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques delivered through an AI-powered coaching interface.

⚠ IMPORTANT: MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

MoodShift is not a medical device and does not provide clinical therapy, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a self-help wellness tool designed to support emotional reflection and cognitive reframing.

If you are experiencing a crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health crisis line immediately.

What we collect

Data stored locally on your device

✓ Stays on your iPhone — never uploaded
  • CBT session conversations and messages
  • Mood scores (1–10) from completed sessions
  • AI memory summaries — only if you enable "AI Coach Personalization"
  • Session history, type, and completion status
  • App preferences (language, theme, disclaimer acceptance)

Data processed by AI (not stored)

Anthropic Claude API

Your messages are processed in real-time by the Claude API to generate coaching responses. This data is not stored or used for AI training under our agreement. We do not use cloud backups or remote storage (no iCloud).

Purchase information

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details. We receive only status confirmation via StoreKit 2.

Subscription plans & data

Free Plan

3 text sessions per week. First 30 days powered by Sonnet 4.6 model; thereafter permanent access via Haiku 4.5 model. No account required. All data local only.

Premium

Unlimited standard text sessions (10 questions per session) powered by Sonnet 4.6. Includes AI Coach Personalization. All conversation data remains strictly local to your device.

Voice AI

Everything in Premium (with Sonnet 4.6 model) plus 30 Voice AI sessions per month. Voice processing (STT/TTS) is performed entirely on-device via Apple frameworks. No audio recordings are ever stored, transmitted, or leave your iPhone.

Your rights & controls

  • Delete sessions: Swipe left on any session in History to delete permanently.
  • Delete all data: Delete the app to remove all local data permanently. Note: Since we do not use cloud backups, deleted data cannot be recovered.
  • Manage subscription: Cancel anytime via App Store → Account → Subscriptions.

Children's privacy

MoodShift is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older (19 in South Korea). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used MoodShift, please contact us.

Questions?

We're a small independent team and we take privacy seriously. Reach out directly if you have questions.
