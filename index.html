Who we are

MoodShift: CBT AI Coach ("we", "our") is an iOS mental wellness application. The App uses Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques delivered through an AI-powered coaching interface.

⚠ IMPORTANT: MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

MoodShift is not a medical device and does not provide clinical therapy, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a self-help wellness tool designed to support emotional reflection.

If you are experiencing a crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health crisis line immediately.

What we collect

Technical Identifiers (Anonymous)

Analytics & Verification

We use an anonymous Device ID (processed via Cloudflare) solely for technical purposes: to verify eligibility for the 30-day Free Trial period and to ensure app stability. This ID is not linked to your identity, as MoodShift does not require user accounts.

Data stored locally on your device

✓ Stays on your iPhone — never uploaded
  • CBT session conversations and messages
  • Mood scores (1–10) from completed sessions
  • AI memory summaries (if enabled)
  • Session history and completion status

Data processed by AI (not stored)

Anthropic Claude API

Your messages are processed in real-time by the Claude API to generate coaching responses. This data is not stored or used for AI training under our agreement. We do not use cloud backups (no iCloud).

Subscription plans & data access

Free Plan

3 sessions per week. First 14 days powered by Sonnet 4.6; thereafter permanent access via the Haiku 4.5 model. No account required. All conversation data is local.

Premium Plan

Includes unlimited text-only sessions (10/10 format) powered by Sonnet 4.6. Includes full access to AI Coach Personalization. Data remains strictly local to your device.

Voice AI Plan

Our most advanced tier. Includes unlimited text sessions (extended 15/15 format) plus 30 Voice AI sessions per month, all powered by Sonnet 4.6. Voice processing is performed entirely on-device via Apple's local frameworks; no audio is ever transmitted or stored.

Your rights & controls

  • Delete data: Swipe left on any session to delete, or delete the App to remove all local data permanently.
  • Manage subscription: Cancel anytime via App Store → Account → Subscriptions.

Children's privacy

MoodShift: CBT AI Coach is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the App, please contact our support team using the details provided at the bottom of this page.
